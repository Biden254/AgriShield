About AgriShield

Empowering farming communities with life-saving flood predictions through the fusion of cutting-edge technology and ancestral wisdom

Our Story

AgriShield was born from the urgent need to protect vulnerable farming communities in flood-prone areas like Budalang'i, Kenya. After witnessing devastating floods that destroyed crops, displaced families, and threatened livelihoods, we realized that traditional early warning systems weren't reaching those who needed them most.

Our innovative approach combines satellite technology with indigenous knowledge systems that communities have relied on for generations. Our Commitment

We believe that technology should serve humanity, especially those most vulnerable to climate change. AgriShield is committed to:

  • Accessibility: Ensuring our services work on basic mobile phones and in low-connectivity areas
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respecting and integrating indigenous knowledge systems
  • Community Ownership: Training local champions to maintain and expand the system
  • Continuous Improvement: Learning from each flood season to enhance our predictions
  • Open Collaboration: Working with governments, NGOs, and research institutions

Looking Forward

Our journey is just beginning. We're working to expand AgriShield to other flood-prone regions across East Africa and beyond. We're also developing features for drought prediction, pest outbreaks, and other climate-related risks that threaten agricultural communities.

Together, we can build a more resilient future where traditional wisdom and modern technology work hand in hand to protect the communities that feed our world.
