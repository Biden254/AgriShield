Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are fundamental to our mission of protecting agricultural communities

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

AgriShield ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our flood early warning system, including our website, mobile application, and related services.

By using AgriShield services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Create an account or register for our services
  • Submit flood observation reports
  • Contact us for support or inquiries
  • Participate in surveys or feedback forms
Data Type Purpose Retention Period

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you submit reports (with your permission)
  • Communication Data: Records of your interactions with our support team

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide personalized flood warnings and alerts
  • Safety: Send emergency notifications and evacuation guidance
  • Improvement: Enhance our prediction algorithms and user experience
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Research: Conduct agricultural resilience and climate adaptation research
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We will share personal information when you give us explicit consent to do so.

4.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our services:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage providers
  • SMS and email delivery services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring tools
  • Payment processors (for premium services)

4.3 Research Partners

We may share anonymized, aggregated data with research institutions and universities studying climate change, agricultural resilience, and disaster risk reduction.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

4.5 Emergency Situations

We may share your information with emergency responders, government agencies, and relief organizations during flood emergencies to protect life and property.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Strict employee access controls and regular security training
  • Infrastructure: Secure cloud hosting with regular security audits
  • Monitoring: 24/7 system monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Backup: Regular data backups to prevent data loss

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Correction

  • View your personal information in your account dashboard
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you

6.2 Data Portability

Request your data in a machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

6.3 Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated data. Note that some information may be retained for research purposes in anonymized form, and we may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6.4 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of non-emergency marketing communications
  • Choose your preferred alert delivery methods
  • Set notification frequency preferences

Important: You cannot opt out of critical flood warnings and emergency alerts, as these are essential for your safety.

7. Children's Privacy

AgriShield services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information from our files.

For users between 13-18 years old, we require parental consent before collecting any personal information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of Kenya. When we transfer your information internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a prominent notice in our mobile app

Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Third-Party Services

Our services may contain links to third-party websites, applications, or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to these third-party services. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.
